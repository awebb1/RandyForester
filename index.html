Magic and Fun For All Occasions

Randy Forster is a magician with a passion for enchantment. Inspired by a mesmerizing card trick at the Yogi Magic Mart as a young man, he now finds himself mesmerizing others. As a member of Dickens Parlour Theatre in the Bethany Beach, Delaware area, Randy's dazzling performances have delighted guests on countless occasions. From his own shows to community events, private functions, and charity galas, Randy's charismatic style appeals to all ages. Experience the Comedy Magic of Randy Forster – a magician for every occasion.
